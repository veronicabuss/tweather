Installing TextBlob for python3.8 (macOS 10.15.7):

From ~ (home dir)
CMD:  python3.8 -m pip install TextBlob
RUN:   /Applications/Python\ 3.8/Install\ Certificates.command 
CMD:  python3.8 -m textblob.download_corpora
